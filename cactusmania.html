fonts and colors

In design, green balances and harmonizes, creating a sense of stability. Green will be appropriate in designs that are associated with prosperity, stability, renewal and nature.

Brighter shades of green are more energetic, while olive shades are usually associated with the natural world. Dark green looks more stable and presentable.
